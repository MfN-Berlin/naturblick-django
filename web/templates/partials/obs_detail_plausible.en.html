Many species are particularly active during a certain period, for example at night. However, this information is not reliable and nocturnal animals can also sometimes be observed during the day. The activity period can therefore only give an indication, but not a reliable confirmation. We refer to Germany for the activity period. Many plants can be found at any time of day and throughout the entire year.
