Naturblick supports your nature experience in the city. The suggestions of the automated species identification are produced by comparing audio recordings of 239 birds.

This means:

  1. only species that have been trained can be recognised,
  2. the recognition works best if there is a high similarity to the recording situation and quality (e.g. microphone quality).

The Naturblick algorithm was trained with audio material from the Animal Sound Archive of the Museum für Naturkunde Berlin, the collaborative online database Xeno-Canto and verified Naturblick recordings. The following species can be identified (September 2021):
