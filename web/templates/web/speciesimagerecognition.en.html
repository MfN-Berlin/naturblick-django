Naturblick supports your nature experience in the city. The suggestions of the automated plant species identification are produced by comparing photos of 2181 plants.

This means:

  1. only species that have been trained can be recognized,
  2. the recognition works best when there is a high similarity to the photo situation and quality (e.g. plants in the outdoors).
